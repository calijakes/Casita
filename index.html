Get to know Casita Las Flores

This impeccable country home is located in peaceful surroundings and benefits from the most amazing views. The layout of the property makes it ideal for anyone who would like to run a business. The main house has 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, the master has a dressing room and en-suite shower. There is a wonderful bright kitchen with sitting area and log burner, the living room has a beautiful high ceiling and fireplace and opens into the huge conservatory currently used as a second living room and dining room. This would be ideal as an exercise or yoga room. In addition, there are 2 annexes. The first one has 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom, living room and kitchen. The second one has a bedroom, bathroom and dressing room. They both open out to the wonderful covered terraces and pool deck. The land is gently sloping with flat areas, which could be suitable for horses.
